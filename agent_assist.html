RealTime Agent Assist "How-to"

Overview

VoiceBase's RealTime Agent Assist coaches agents through customer interactions with smart in-app guidance. VoiceBase customers may set up the feature in their account by first creating Categories based on the agent's script and/or legal disclaimers, and using these Categories to build Checklists. The Checklists are the helper tools for the agents, meaning that as the conversation unfolds with a caller, the Checklists are marked either as complete or incomplete. Alerts may be set to capture inappropriate language or other reminders, and timely Global Popups coach agents with correct wording for product features, billing language, or any other helpful information they may need during their call.

Create Categories

  • Training on how to create Categories may be accessed here.
  • In your VoiceBase account, go to Analyst Tools > Categories > Add Category
  • Create Categories per agent's script, legal or other disclaimers, and any other required content.
  • Create Categories filtering for impolite or foul language that you can use later for Alerts.
  • Create Categories to hit on language surrounding products, billing, or rebuttals to trigger a Global Popup to coach agents during a call.

Build and Manage Checklists

  • In your VoiceBase account, go to RealTime > Checklists > Add Checklist
  • Choose a Name and External Id for each Checklist.
  • Add a Global Popup to your Checklist to coach agents on overcoming resistance or using correct wording for products.
  • Choose a Category from the dropdown menu that will hit on words in the call to trigger your Popup.
  • Add an Alert to your Checklist, give it a name, and choose the Category for Alerts from the dropdown menu.
  • Create either a Hard or Soft alert message.
  • Add a List Item to your Checklist and give it a name such as "Introduction".
  • Choose the corresponding Category for this List Item from the dropdown menu of Categories.
  • In the "Description" box for the List Item, insert the appropriate part of your agent script.
