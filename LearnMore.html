In order to focus on our API-based business, VoiceBase will be transferring
this web app to longtime business partner cielo24 on 11/1/2018.

The web app site at voicebase.com will shut down on 10/31/2018.
The new site at cielo24.com will be available 11/1/2018.

The VoiceBase V3 API is unaffected and will continue operating unchanged.


What are my options?


API customers, our services are unchanged.

  • If you are an API customer please return to our developer site
    and choose the options under 'Developer'.

Web app users, please note the options below.

  • Unless you choose otherwise, your account and content
    will be transferred and will be available here on 11/1.
  • You may download a copy of all your content here.
  • If you do not wish to have your account transferred you may cancel your account on the
    "My Account" page.
  • All your content will be deleted after you confirm the cancellation.
