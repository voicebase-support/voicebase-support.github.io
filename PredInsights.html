Is there a general timetable for when insights are returned? What is the ETA difference between the standard and premium engine?

There are a few factors that determine turnaround time. Generally premium takes about 1.5x standard. You would also need to consider your priority level - low, normal, or high, as well as the additional features you want to have processed. Generally keywords, predictions, PCI, etc only add a few seconds to each job, but that may be a meaningful addo-on for voicemail. "Scheduled" is the second status after "accepted". After scheduled we will process, then it will finish. The exact turnaround time is based on the SLA you set up with us. For voicemail we make very fast SLAs, but for archived content we can make slower SLAs to give you a better price.
