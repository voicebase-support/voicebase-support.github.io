Introduction to the VoiceBase Query Language and Analytics Workbench

This VoiceBase Query Language Guide is designed to get you up to speed and familiar with VoiceBase and walk you through the basic usage of both the data query tool called the Analytics Workbench and the VoiceBase Query Language (VBQL). VoiceBase is a state-of-the-art Voice Analytics platform that allows you to upload, analyze, categorize and optimize insights around voice and messaging data. VBQL allows you to write powerful, flexible Queries to access and filter your data, and you'll do all your analysis through the Analytics Workbench.

To access the Analytics Workbench, login to your VoiceBase account and go to Analyst Tools > Workbench.
